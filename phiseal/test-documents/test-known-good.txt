## SERVICE AGREEMENT

**Parties:**
- Provider: TechCorp Inc., registered in Delaware (Company ID: 12345)
- Client: Johnson Enterprises LLC, registered in California (Company ID: 67890)

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Term:** 12 months from Effective Date
**Termination Date:** December 31, 2025

**Services Provided:**
1. Software Development Services

   - Database design and implementation
   - API integration services

2. Maintenance Services
   - Bug fixes within 48 hours of report
   - Security updates quarterly
   - Performance optimization as needed

**Payment Terms:**
- Monthly Fee: $10,000 USD
- Payment Due: 5th business day of each month
- Late Payment Fee: 2% per month on overdue amounts

- Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Database design and API framework
- Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Frontend development
- Phase 3 (Months 7-9): Integration and testing
- Phase 4 (Months 10-12): Deployment and training

**Intellectual Property:**
- All work product becomes property of Client upon full payment
- Provider retains rights to pre-existing tools and frameworks
- Client grants Provider right to use project as portfolio example

**Confidentiality:**
- Both parties agree to protect confidential information
- Confidentiality obligations survive contract termination by 3 years
- Confidential information excludes publicly available data

**Liability:**
- Provider liability capped at total fees paid in preceding 12 months
- Force majeure clause applies for natural disasters, war, pandemic
- Each party maintains insurance coverage of minimum $1 million

- Good faith negotiation for 30 days
- Mediation in Delaware before litigation
